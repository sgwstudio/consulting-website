About

I'm a Freelance Merchandising & Product Strategy Consultant based in Los Angeles, with over fifteen years of experience shaping luxury and global lifestyle brands. I specialize in building merchandising strategies that drive growth across product assortment, new category development, design consulting, and vintage sourcing, while ensuring brands meet both consumer needs and business goals.

Most recently, I led the New Product Categories unit at Jacques Marie Mage, overseeing Jewelry and Leather Goods from design inception through to sales execution. Previously, I directed assortments for Vans' Women's & Kids Footwear and Apparel across the Americas, managed global retail merchandising at Chloé, and bought accessories collections at Lanvin.

With expertise in product strategy, market analysis, and trend forecasting, I help brands create collections that resonate across diverse global markets. Fluent in English, French, and Italian, I bring cultural fluency and strategic focus to every project.
